See how attacker find flaws in web browser plugins to install malware to your computer. For example, if a plugin has vulnerable readFile/loadFile function, then he can read/load any files from your computer and then send them to his sever. Similarly, for saveFile function, he can overwrite any files on your disk with malicious content.
