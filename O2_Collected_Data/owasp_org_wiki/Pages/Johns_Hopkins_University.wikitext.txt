===OWASP Johns Hopkins University===

Welcome to the '''Johns Hopkins University''' Chapter of the [http://www.owasp.org Open Web Application Security Project]. This is an attempt to bring together all the Application Security Enthusiasts within and around the university together; to provide them with a platform to discuss, and procure all the latest and greatest in the field of application security. 

'''Note:''' We are not official yet. I need to get the necessary permissions from our director inorder to hold our first official meeting. Keep checking for latest updates. 

===Google Summer of Code=== 

Every security enthusiast must be thrilled as OWASP has been accepted as a [[http://code.google.com/soc/ Google Summer of Code]] mentor. If you have interesting ideas for the project or want to contribute to the existing projects, you can take a peek at the list here [[https://www.owasp.org/index.php/GSoC2012_Ideas#Information_for_Students OWASP Google Summer of Code]]. 

If you are a code guru or aspiring to be one, this is your thing. '''Code the Summer Away!'''
