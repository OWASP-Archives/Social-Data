 ! align="center" style="background:#4058A0; color:white" | T3. Advanced ASP.NET Exploits and Countermeasures - One Day Course - October 16th, 2006
 |-
 | style="background:#F2F2F2" | In this one day course you will push ASP.NET to the limit and will be shown how ASP.NET applications and environments can be exploited by skilled attackers. Advanced exploitation techniques will be presented together with low-level technical analysis of the .NET Framework. You will also learn advanced defense techniques such as: Building an ASP.NET Security Protection layer (also called a Web Application Firewall) and Real time patching of vulnerabilities in the target application, the .NET Framework or the CLR.
