== Biography ==
Christian Heinrich is a Project Leader of the OWASP "Google Hacking" Project and contributed to the "Spiders/Robots/Crawlers" and "Search Engine Reconnaissance" sections of the OWASP Testing Guide v3 and presented at the recent OWASP Australia Conference and will be presenting at the upcoming [http://sandiego.toorcon.org/ ToorCon X in San Diego on 26 - 28 September] and [http://www.sector.ca SecTor 2008 in Toronto, Canada on 7 - 8 October 2008]. 

Christian Heinrich has a Public Profile on LinkedIn at http://www.linkedin.com/in/ChristianHeinrich and can be reached at [mailto:owasp@cmlh.id.au owasp@cmlh.id.au]

== Abstract ==
The September 2008 Update of the OWASP "Google Hacking" Project will demonstrate the "Spiders/Robots/Crawlers" and "Search Engine Reconnaissance" sections of the OWASP Testing Guide v3 and the "DownloadIndexedCache" Proof of Concept (PoC) which implements the Google SOAP Search API to retrieve content indexed within the Google Cache.
