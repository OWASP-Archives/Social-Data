'''Roshen Chandran''' is the Director of Paladion's Application Security Practice that has tested 1000+ applications. Roshen's team assists enterprises set up and run their enterprise security testing initiatives. Roshen is the co-author of "Know Your Enemy" from Addison Wesley. He has spoken at the RSA Conference, Cansecwest and SDWest Conferences.
