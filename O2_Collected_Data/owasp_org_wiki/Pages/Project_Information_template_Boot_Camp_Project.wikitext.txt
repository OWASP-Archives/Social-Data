 | style="width:15%; background:#7B8ABD" align="center"| '''Short Project Description''' 
 | colspan="7" style="width:85%; background:#cccccc" align="left"|The project will gather continuously boot camp material about OWASP tooling and documentation. The aim is to create an easy entrance towards the OWASP tooling and education projects. With the boot camp slide decks the OWASP projects can be presented, both inside and outside of the OWASP community, in a standardized and controlled manner. The boot camp slide decks have to be delivered by the projects will be reviewed by the Boot Camp Project. 
'''The OWASP Boot Camp Project is a sub-project of the Education Project and a deliverable of the Global Education Committee!'''
