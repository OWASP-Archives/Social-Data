== Calendars of OWASP events in Slovakia ==

We are starting regular OWASP WebAppSec meetings in Bratislava - don't hesitate and come!

'''Where:''' [http://www.progressbar.sk/en/location Progressbar hackerspace]

'''When:''' the 1st Thursday of every month

'''What:''' WebAppSec presentations, lightning talks, worksessions, OWASP projects
