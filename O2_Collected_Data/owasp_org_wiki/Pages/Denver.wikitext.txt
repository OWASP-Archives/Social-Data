====Hands-on Introduction to WebScarab & WebGoat====
 
 Please bring a laptop that has wireless capability with a copy of WebScarab & Firefox installed.
Tim Van Cleave will introduce WebScarab and show and tell us how to use it on WebGoat.
The content will be geared towards those who have little to no experience using a web proxy or application testing  and want to learn.  Tim will cover some of the OWASP Top 10 issues to get students started using a proxy and web application security testing.
