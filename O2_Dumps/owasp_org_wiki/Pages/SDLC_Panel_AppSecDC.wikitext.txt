== Topics of Discussion  ==

A discussion of Software Development Life Cycle implementations by industry leading experts.

This panel will be moderated by Pravir Chandra, Leader of the [http://www.opensamm.org/ OpenSAMM] project.

== The Panelists  ==

* Dan Cornell -- [http://www.denimgroup.com/ Denim Group]
* Michael Craigue -- [http://www.dell.com/ Dell Computers]
* Dennis Hurst -- [http://www.hp.com Hewlett Packard]
* Joey Peloquin -- [http://www.fishnetsecurity.com/ FishNet Security]
* Keith Turpin -- [http://www.boeing.com/ The Boeing Company]

== Format ==

* Introduction by Pravir Chandra discussing the concept and importance of software security assurance
* Introduction of panelists and presentation of brief abstracts
* Moderated Question and Answer, led by Pravir
* Open Question and Answer, led by Pravir
* Closing Remarks.
