Kelly provides operational support for the Global Membership Committee.  Her job duties include: 

Taking and posting committee meeting minutes 
Follow up with committee members re: committee deliverable's and initiatives 
Editing and updating committee wiki page 
Editing and proofing committee documents 
Facilitating meetings/calls/working relationships with key personnel 
Improving integrity of Membership database
Other various administrative tasks as assigned by Committee Chair and/or Operations Director
