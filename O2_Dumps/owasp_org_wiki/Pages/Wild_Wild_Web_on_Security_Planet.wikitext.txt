Organisms keep themselves safe in a world that's every bit as unpredictable as our world. This presentation will parallel what we can learn from the natural world and apply it to the world of security (web application security included). For e.g., What does the Stick Insects' regenerative ability have to do with software 'bugs' that show themselves after being fixed - poor configuration management? or can the Ostrich head in the sand behavior reflect on the modicum of risk management today and many more ...
The talk would be a lighthearted fun-filled, extremely interactive session covering various concepts of security from risk management, defense in depth, secure programming, threats and vulnerabilities and compliance and more ...
