Chapter Accounts are maintained and managed by OWASP Foundation

Quick balance is available online.  Detailed account lines are available by [mailto:accounting@owasp.org request]

Chapter's whos balance exceeds $3000 must 

* have a named treasurer listed on their chapter page

* submit an annual budget

Chapter funds in excess of $1000 will expire 12 months from the date of aquesition unless deemed necessary by a future budget expense
