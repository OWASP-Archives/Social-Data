'''Talk: An Introduction to the OWASP Top Ten'''

'''Slides: [[File:OWASP_Manchester_12_02_10_Top_Ten.pptx]] '''

The OWASP Top Ten provides a powerful awareness document for web application security. The OWASP Top Ten represents a broad consensus about what the most critical web application security flaws are. 

In this presentation Simon will give an introduction to each of the top 10 security flaws and explain why they are so dangerous.

'''Speaker: [https://www.owasp.org/index.php/User:Simon_Bennetts Simon Bennetts]'''


'''Talk: Evaluating iOS Applications'''

'''Slides: [[File:Evaluating_iOS_Applications.pdf]] '''

In this presentation, Dominic will discuss the lessons learned from evaluating iOS applications. 
It will cover a brief introduction to the security features of the platform and how they affect mobile applications then delve in to details on some of the most common iOS vulnerabilities the author has experienced during testing.

'''Speaker: Dominic Chell - [http://www.mdsec.co.uk/ MDSec] '''

Dominic is the co-owner and director of MDSec; a security consultancy based in the UK. Prior to this, Dominic worked at NGS Software for 6 years where he was a CHECK Team Leader & CREST consultant. Dominic has published numerous exploits and advisories over the years as well as given web application security training at BlackHat Vegas.
