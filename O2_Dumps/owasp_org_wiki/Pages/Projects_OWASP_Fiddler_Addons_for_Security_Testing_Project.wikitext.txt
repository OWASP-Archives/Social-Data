*The '''OWASP Fiddler Addons for Security Testing Project''' (aka OWASP FAST) is the umbrella for two complementary projects:
** '''[[Projects/OWASP Watcher Project|Watcher Project]]''' - a passive vulnerability scanner,
** '''[[Projects/OWASP X5s Project|X5s Project]]''' - an active XSS testing and input/output encoding detection.
